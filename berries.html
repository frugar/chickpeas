All Natural, No Pesticide Berries!

We have more than 30,000 strawberry, blueberry and raspberry plants condensed into 17 acres, we produce upwards of 100,000 lbs of berries each year, and are the largest all natural, no pesticide U-Pick Berry Farm along the east coast of Florida. We are conveniently located just minutes off of I-95 in the lovely town of Mims, FL.

Berry season typically runs from the beginning of April through mid June, and people from all over Florida come to our Farm to pick berries and enjoy a great day out on the Farm. Most U-Pick farms have bushes that are taller and only adults can reach the bulk of the berries. Not us! Our bushes are trimmed so that kids can easily reach the berries.

Children also love our convenient tractor pulled hay wagon that travels around the property to make it fast and easy to go from field to field. Enjoy your berry picking, and have fast and convenient transport to delicious veggie harvesting and on to collecting amazing pasture raised eggs!
