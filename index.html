Organic Eggs, Vegetables & Berries

The Largest U-Pick Natural Farm in Florida

We have more than 30 acres of vegetables growing year round, nearly 20 acres of berries, and hundreds of pasture raised chickens that provide a daily abundance of eggs. We never use any pestecides or artifical chemicals of any kind and we practice ethical and sustainable farming practices. We are conveniently located just minutes off of I-95 in the lovely town of Mims, FL.
